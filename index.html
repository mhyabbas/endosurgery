Dr. Prathima Chowdary

Welcome to Endosurgery

Dr Prathima Chowdary is a Specialist Gynaecologist located in the city of Auckland. She provides specialist care in Endometriosis, Laparoscopic procedures including Hysterectomy and Myomectomy, Robotic Procedures, Hysteroscopic procedures including Endometrial Ablation and General Gynaecology. She also performs some obstetrics procedures including Laparoscopic Cervical Stitch Insertion, and Caesarian Scar Repair, laparoscopic myomectomies, laparoscopic hysterectomy for large fibroid uterus and is accredited morcellation.

The patient comes first with Dr Chowdary. Her main focus is patient care and she takes great pride in actively listening to patients and responding to their needs. She is committed to ensuring that each patient receives the most up-to-date and thorough medical investigation and treatment available.
